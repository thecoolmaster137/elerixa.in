Key Features

  1. Manpower - Own presence at site or 1 Dedicated Site Engineer. Who should have very good knowledge of work quality and safety.
  2. Installation of all solar project components on turnkey basis: All material unloading, shifting, and movement within the site is under EEPL scope for all items. (machineries which will be needed for such work will be provided by supplier or company or party, for e.g. Earth pit auguring and boring, Hydra/Crane, Forklift [Module shifting] or any other machinery required during execution).
  3. Tools - Required tools for various operations are under EEPL scope.
  4. Safety - Labour safety under EEPL scope.
  5. Module damages during Installation - Labour to be properly instructed with regards to handling of Modules. Any incidents identified for damaging of modules will have to be compensated at actuals by EEPL.
  6. Timelines - As per mutual understanding of EEPL and concern supplier or company or party.
